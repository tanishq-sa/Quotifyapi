Cancellation Policy

Last updated: December 2026

1 Cancellation Overview

You can cancel your Quotify API subscription at any time. This policy outlines the different ways to cancel and what happens when you do.

🔄 Immediate Effect

Cancellation takes effect immediately

📧 Easy Process

Simple email-based cancellation

2 How to Cancel Your Subscription

1

Email Us

Send cancellation request to support@dazzelr.tech

2

Provide Details

Include your account email and reason

3

Confirmation

Receive confirmation within 24 hours

3 What Happens When You Cancel

Immediate Downgrade

Your account is immediately downgraded to the Free plan

API Access Continues

You can still use the API with Free plan limits (50 requests/day)

No More Billing

No further charges will be made to your payment method

Data Retention

Your account data and API keys are preserved

4 Types of Cancellation

🔄 Temporary Pause

Pause your subscription for a specific period

  • • Downgrade to Free plan
  • • Resume anytime
  • • No data loss

❌ Permanent Cancel

Completely cancel your subscription

  • • Downgrade to Free plan
  • • No automatic renewal
  • • Manual reactivation required

5 Reactivating Your Subscription

You can reactivate your subscription at any time by upgrading to a paid plan again. The process is simple and your account will be restored to the selected plan immediately.

🔄 Easy Reactivation

Visit our pricing page and select a plan

⚡ Instant Access

Your plan is activated immediately after payment

6 Data and Account Information

Account Preserved

Your account, API keys, and usage history remain intact

Data Export

You can export your data before or after cancellation

Account Deletion

You can request complete account deletion if needed

7 Need Help with Cancellation?

If you need assistance with cancelling your subscription or have questions about the process, please contact us:
